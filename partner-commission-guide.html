Attribution Rules

We use first-touch attribution with a 90-day cookie window. Here is exactly how it works:

  • First-touch wins. The first partner whose referral link the client clicks gets attribution. No splitting.
  • 90-day window. When a client clicks your link, you own that email address for 90 days. Any purchase within that window is yours.
  • Another partner clicked first? If the client already clicked a different partner's link before yours, that partner gets attribution. First-touch, no exceptions.
  • Already a Caugia customer? If the client had an existing Caugia account and purchases before clicking your link, you do not earn commission on pre-existing purchases. You earn commission on new purchases only.
  • Ambiguous attribution? If attribution is genuinely unclear, Caugia decides. Our default is to favor the partner.

Edge Cases

When You DO Get Paid

  • Client upgrades from Core to Full — you earn 20% of the new, higher amount
  • Client adds module assessments — you earn 20% on every add-on purchase
  • Client renews after pausing — you earn 20% when payments resume
  • Client's colleague signs up under the same company — attributed to you if the same email domain, within the 90-day window

When You DON'T Get Paid

  • Client was already a paying Caugia customer before your referral — no commission on pre-existing purchases
  • Client clicked another partner's link first — first-touch wins, no exceptions
  • Client requests a refund within 14 days — commission is reversed
  • Client's subscription is paused — commission paused (resumes when payment resumes)
  • Client disputes the charge (chargeback) — commission is reversed
  • Partner violates terms (spam, misrepresentation) — account suspended, commissions forfeited

Commissions follow a predictable monthly cycle. No surprises.

  • Calculation: 1st of each month, covering the previous month's purchases and renewals.
  • Approval: 1st through 10th. We verify no refunds, chargebacks, or disputes occurred.
  • Payout: 15th of each month via bank transfer.
  • Minimum payout: €50. Smaller amounts roll over to the next month.
  • Currency: Payouts are in EUR. Non-EUR transactions are converted at Stripe's exchange rate on the date of the original charge.

Tax & Legal

Partners are independent contractors, not employees. The commission relationship does not constitute employment, agency, or partnership with Caugia SASU.

You are responsible for your own tax obligations in your jurisdiction. Caugia does not withhold taxes on commission payouts.

Caugia provides annual commission statements in January for the previous calendar year.

If your monthly payouts exceed €500, you must provide valid invoicing details (company name, address, VAT number if applicable) for compliance purposes.
