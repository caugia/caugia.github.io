Attribution

How attribution works

First-touch attribution with a 90-day cookie window. Simple, transparent, and designed to reward the partner who initiates the relationship.

First-touch wins

The first partner whose referral link the client clicks gets attribution. No splitting.

90-day window

You own that email address for 90 days after the click. Any purchase within the window is yours.

Other partner first?

First-touch applies. That partner gets attribution, no exceptions.

Existing customer?

No commission on pre-existing purchases. You earn on new purchases only.

Ambiguous?

Caugia decides. Our intent is to resolve ambiguity in the partner's favor where possible.

Edge Cases

When you do. and don't. get paid

Full transparency. No hidden clauses, no surprises.

When You DO Get Paid

Upgrade Core → Full You earn your tier rate on the new, higher amount
Client adds modules You earn your tier rate on every add-on purchase
Renews after pausing You earn your tier rate when payments resume
Colleague same company Attributed to you if same email domain, within 90-day window

When You DON'T Get Paid

Already a customer No commission on pre-existing purchases
Other partner first First-touch wins, no exceptions
Refund within 14 days Commission is reversed
Subscription paused Commission paused (resumes when payment resumes)
Chargeback Commission is reversed
Terms violation Account suspended, commissions forfeited
