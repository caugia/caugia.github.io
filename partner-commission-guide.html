Attribution Rules

We use first-touch attribution with a 90-day cookie window. Here is exactly how it works:

First-touch winsThe first partner whose referral link the client clicks gets attribution. No splitting. 90-day windowWhen a client clicks your link, you own that email address for 90 days. Any purchase within that window is yours. Other partner first?That partner gets attribution. First-touch, no exceptions. Existing customer?No commission on pre-existing purchases. You earn commission on new purchases only. Ambiguous?Caugia decides. Our intent is to resolve ambiguity in the partner's favor where possible.
