Partner Commission Guide

How commissions work, what qualifies, and how much you can earn.

Commission Rate

20%
Recurring commission on all products

What Qualifies

You earn commission on every product your referred clients purchase:

  • GTM Intelligence Reports
  • GRIP module assessments
  • GRIP OS Core subscriptions
  • GRIP OS Full subscriptions
  • Bundles and custom packages

Attribution

First-touch, 90-day window.

When your client clicks your referral link, a 90-day attribution cookie is set. Any purchase made within that window is attributed to you. If the client previously visited caugia.com without a referral link, your link takes attribution priority.

Payment Schedule

Commissions are calculated monthly. Payouts are processed on the 15th of the following month via bank transfer.

Status Tracking

Each commission moves through three stages:

Pending Purchase made
→
Approved Payment cleared, no refund
→
Paid Transferred to you

Dashboard

Track referrals, attribution, and commission status at os.caugia.com/partner.

Tax Information

Partners are responsible for their own tax obligations. Caugia issues annual commission statements for your records.

Lifetime Commission

No cap. No cliff.

Your commission continues for as long as the client remains a paying customer. There is no maximum payout and no expiration on recurring commissions.
