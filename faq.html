1. Before You Begin

How long does the assessment take to complete? ▼
The assessment typically takes 45-60 minutes to complete. However, this depends on how readily you have access to your metrics and whether you need to consult with teammates or systems.
Can I complete it in multiple sessions? ▼
Yes! Your progress is automatically saved every few seconds. You can close the page and return anytime to pick up exactly where you left off. Your answers remain saved in your browser.
Do I need access to CRM or analytics tools while answering? ▼
It's helpful but not required. Many questions ask for specific metrics (CAC, NRR, pipeline coverage, etc.). Having access to your CRM, analytics dashboard, or recent reports will make the process faster and more accurate.
What information should I have ready before starting? ▼
Recommended cheat sheet:
• Current ARR, ACV, and revenue targets
• CAC, LTV, NRR, churn rate
• Win rate, sales cycle length, pipeline coverage
• Team sizes (sales, marketing, CS, RevOps)
• Key conversion rates (MQL→SQL, SQL→Won)
• Growth metrics (YoY growth, expansion rate)
Should multiple people fill this in together or individually? ▼
It depends on your preference. Some leaders prefer to complete it solo with data on hand, while others find it valuable to work through it with their RevOps or GTM leadership team to ensure accuracy and alignment on how things actually work.
Can I delegate parts of the assessment to teammates? ▼
Technically yes, but we don't recommend it. The assessment is designed to be completed by one person (typically a GTM leader) who has visibility across the entire revenue engine. Delegating sections can lead to fragmented or inconsistent responses.

2. Completing the Assessment

How do I save my progress? ▼
Progress is saved automatically every few seconds as you type or select answers. There's also a "Save" button you can click manually if you want to ensure your latest answers are stored before closing the page.
Can I pause and resume later? ▼
Absolutely. You can close the browser at any time and return later. Your progress is saved locally in your browser, so you'll pick up right where you left off on the same device.
Can I go back and change answers? ▼
Yes! Use the "Previous" button to navigate back to earlier questions. You can change any answer at any time before final submission.
Can I skip questions and come back later? ▼
Yes, you can leave questions blank and move forward. However, certain questions are required to progress (marked clearly in the interface). You can always use the pillar navigation on the right to jump between sections.
What happens if I close the page? ▼
Nothing is lost. Your progress is saved in your browser's local storage. When you return and open the assessment again, you'll see exactly where you left off.
What if I don't know an answer? ▼
You have two options: (1) provide your best estimate, or (2) leave it blank. If you're unsure, it's better to estimate than to skip—approximations still provide valuable diagnostic signals. Just avoid wild guesses.
How precise do my answers need to be? ▼
Approximate accuracy is fine. For example, if your churn rate is "somewhere between 4% and 6%", entering 5% is perfectly acceptable. The goal is directional accuracy, not forensic precision.
What if we don't track this metric yet? ▼
That's valuable information in itself. If you don't track something like CAC or NRR, you can either leave it blank or provide an educated guess. The fact that it's not tracked will be reflected in your maturity assessment.
Should I estimate or leave a question blank? ▼
If you can make a reasonable estimate, do so. Blank answers reduce the diagnostic value of the assessment. However, if you truly have no basis for an estimate, leaving it blank is better than guessing wildly.
Will leaving questions blank affect my score? ▼
Yes, but only in that pillar. Blank answers mean we have less data to assess that specific area of your GTM system. Your overall GTM Score will still be calculated based on the questions you do answer.
Will my answers be visible to my team or colleagues? ▼
No. Your answers are private and only visible to you and Caugia Consulting. They are not shared with anyone else unless you explicitly choose to share your final report.

3. Understanding the Questions

What does a "maturity level" represent? ▼
Maturity levels (often 1-5 scales) represent how developed, structured, or sophisticated a particular GTM capability is. Higher maturity typically means more process, documentation, predictability, and operational rigor.
Are there right or wrong answers? ▼
No. This is a diagnostic, not a test. There are no "correct" answers—only accurate or inaccurate reflections of your current reality. Honesty is far more valuable than trying to look good.
Why do some questions feel similar? ▼
Some questions may seem overlapping because GTM systems are interconnected. For example, sales execution and revenue operations both touch pipeline management. The slight differences in wording help us triangulate a more accurate picture.
What if our situation doesn't match any option exactly? ▼
Choose the option that's closest to your reality. GTM systems are messy and don't always fit neat categories. Pick the answer that best represents your current state, even if it's not a perfect match.
What if different teams work differently in our organization? ▼
Answer based on the most common or representative state across your organization. If there's significant variation (e.g., EMEA operates very differently from North America), choose the answer that best reflects your primary or largest segment.

4. Technical Issues

My answers aren't saving — what should I do? ▼
First, check that your browser allows local storage (most do by default). If you're in private/incognito mode, your progress may not save. Try switching to a regular browser window. If issues persist, contact support.
The page froze or crashed — will I lose progress? ▼
No. Progress is saved continuously in your browser. Simply refresh the page or reopen the assessment, and you'll be back where you left off. If you encounter persistent crashes, contact support with details about your browser and device.
I clicked Reset by accident — can this be undone? ▼
Unfortunately, no. The Reset button clears all saved progress immediately. There's no undo function. If this happens, you'll need to restart the assessment from the beginning. We recommend being very careful with that button!
The next/previous buttons aren't working. ▼
This may happen if you haven't answered a required question. Check that all required fields on the current question have been filled in. If the issue persists, try refreshing the page or contact support.
Dark mode isn't working on my browser. ▼
Dark mode should work on all modern browsers. If the toggle button isn't responding, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. Supported browsers include Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions).
Does this work on mobile? ▼
Yes, the assessment is fully responsive and works on mobile devices. However, we recommend using a desktop or tablet for the best experience, especially since you may need to reference other tools or documents while answering.
Which browsers are fully supported? ▼
The assessment works best on the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser up to date for the smoothest experience. Internet Explorer is not supported.

5. After Submission

When will I receive my GTM Intelligence Report? ▼
You'll receive your comprehensive GTM Intelligence Report within 5-7 business days after submission. The report is custom-built based on your specific answers and includes detailed diagnostics, archetype classification, and strategic recommendations.
What happens immediately after I submit? ▼
You'll receive a confirmation email acknowledging your submission. Our team then begins analyzing your responses, mapping them to our GTM Framework, identifying patterns, and building your custom report.
Can I download or revisit my answers later? ▼
After submission, your answers are locked and stored securely. You won't be able to edit them, but you can request a copy of your raw answers by contacting support. Your final report will include the key data points you provided.
Will I see my GTM Score right away? ▼
No. Your GTM Score is calculated as part of the full analysis process and will be included in your final report. It's not generated instantly because it requires human review and contextual interpretation of your answers.
Can I share my report with my team? ▼
Absolutely. Once you receive your report, you own it and can share it with anyone you choose—your leadership team, board, investors, or advisors. Many clients use it as a strategic planning tool with their GTM leadership.
What format will the report be in? ▼
Your GTM Intelligence Report will be delivered as a comprehensive PDF document, typically 30-50 pages. It includes visual diagnostics, detailed pillar assessments, your GTM Archetype classification, and actionable recommendations.
How are my answers analyzed? ▼
Your responses are mapped to our proprietary GTM Framework, which evaluates 12 pillars across 4 dimensions (Guidance, Resources, Implementation, Performance). We use pattern recognition, benchmarking, and expert analysis to identify strengths, gaps, and risks.
Is my data stored securely? ▼
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow industry-standard security practices and never share your data with third parties. Your information is used solely to generate your custom report and improve our diagnostic models (in anonymized, aggregated form).
Can I redo the assessment in the future? ▼
Yes. Many clients retake the assessment 6-12 months later to measure progress. Each assessment is treated as a separate engagement and requires a new purchase. Comparing reports over time provides valuable insight into GTM evolution.

6. Getting Help

How do I contact support during the assessment? ▼
Email us at support@caugia.com with your question or issue. Include details about which question you're on and what you need help with. We typically respond within 4-6 business hours.
Can I schedule a call if I'm unsure about a question? ▼
Yes. If you're stuck or need clarification, you can book a 15-minute support call via our contact page. This is especially helpful if you're uncertain about how to interpret specific questions in your context.
Who do I contact if something seems incorrect in the report? ▼
Email support@caugia.com with specific details about what seems off. We'll review your report and answers to ensure accuracy. In rare cases, we may need to regenerate parts of the report if there was an analytical error.
Is there a glossary for GTM terminology? ▼
Yes. Your final GTM Intelligence Report includes a comprehensive glossary of all terms and metrics used throughout the assessment. If you need definitions during the assessment, email support and we'll clarify immediately.
What's the expected response time for support? ▼
We aim to respond to all support inquiries within 4-6 business hours. During European business hours (9am-6pm CET), you can often expect responses within 1-2 hours. For urgent technical issues, we prioritize accordingly.
