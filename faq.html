Assessment Support

Everything you need to know about completing the GTM Intelligence Report.

1. Before You Begin

How long does the assessment take to complete?
Most people complete the assessment in 45–60 minutes. If you already know your core metrics (CAC, NRR, win rates, pipeline coverage), it may go faster.
Can I complete it in multiple sessions?
Yes. Your progress is saved automatically in your browser as you answer questions. You can close the page and return at any time.
Do I need access to CRM or analytics tools?
Not necessarily, but it helps. Some questions ask for specific metrics, and having your CRM or analytics dashboard available will make the process faster and more accurate.
What information should I have ready?
A simple cheat sheet makes the experience smoother. Recommended metrics include:
  • ARR, ACV and revenue targets
  • CAC, LTV, NRR, churn rate
  • Win rate, sales cycle length, pipeline coverage
  • Team sizes across marketing, sales, CS and RevOps
Directional numbers are fine; perfect precision is not required.
Should multiple people fill this in together?
Either works. Some leaders fill it in solo with data at hand. Others complete it with RevOps or the broader GTM leadership team to ensure full alignment.

2. Completing the Assessment

How do I save my progress?
Your progress is saved automatically every few seconds using your browser's local storage. You do not need to press a save button.
Can I go back and change answers?
Yes. You can use the "Back" button to navigate to previous steps and update any answer before submitting the assessment.
What if I don't know an answer?
You will never be forced to guess. Every question includes fallback options such as "We do not track this yet" or "Limited visibility". Choosing these is diagnostically meaningful.
How precise do my answers need to be?
They do not need to be perfect. Directional accuracy is enough (e.g. 5% churn vs 4.8%). If you cannot estimate, choose the fallback option.
Why can't I leave questions blank?
Blank answers provide no diagnostic value. We need to know *why* the data is missing (e.g. not tracked vs not applicable) to build your maturity profile correctly.

3. Understanding the Questions

What does a "maturity level" represent?
Maturity levels describe how structured, reliable and consistent a capability is. They range from reactive (hero-based) to predictable (system-based).
Are there right or wrong answers?
No. There is only an accurate reflection of your current state. Being honest about gaps is the only way to get a roadmap that actually solves them.
What if none of the options describe our situation perfectly?
Choose the option that best approximates reality. GTM environments are complex; pick the answer that is "most true" for your primary revenue motion.

4. Technical Issues

My answers aren't saving.
Check that you are not in "Private" or "Incognito" mode, as this often blocks local storage. Switch to a regular window to ensure progress is saved.
The page froze, will I lose progress?
Unlikely. Because progress saves continuously, you can refresh the page or close/reopen the tab without losing your work.
Does this work on mobile?
Yes, but for the best experience (referencing data/metrics), we recommend completing the assessment on a desktop or tablet.

5. After Submission

When will I receive my report?
Within 5–7 business days. The report involves an AI-assisted analysis and a quality review by our team.
Can I download my answers later?
After submission, the assessment is closed. If you need a copy of your raw data inputs, please contact support.
Will I see my GTM Score immediately?
No. Unlike the free tool, the Intelligence Report calculates a deeper, weighted score that is delivered as part of the full PDF analysis.

6. Getting Help

How do I contact support?
Email support@caugia.com. We typically respond within 4–6 business hours (European time zone).
Can I schedule a clarification call?
Yes. If you are stuck on a specific section, you can request a brief clarification call via the contact page.
