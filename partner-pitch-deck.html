The Partner Funnel

Five steps. This is the canonical sequence. Do not skip steps or reorder them.

1
Refer
Share your partner link. The client lands on caugia.com with 90-day attribution tied to your account. You do not need to be in the room. The attribution is automatic.
2
Diagnose
The client completes the GTM Intelligence Report. ~35 minutes, 265 questions. The system runs 72 scoring engines across 12 pillars and 4 dimensions (G, R, I, P). Alternatively, you buy the report for the client — this works especially well for consultants and portfolio operators.
3
Review
Sit with the client. Walk through the GRIP scores. Focus on two things: the binding constraint and the financial leakage number. Do not try to explain everything. The constraint is the conversation.
4
Convert
Recommend GRIP OS for ongoing constraint governance. Core (€799/mo) for teams that want self-service execution. Full (€1,495/mo) for teams that want Sophie as their GTM advisor plus the full platform with 340+ connectors, benchmarks, and the simulator.
5
Expand
As the client resolves the binding constraint, a new one emerges. Each constraint cycle deepens the relationship, proves ROI, and extends your commission. The system is designed for ongoing governance, not one-time fixes.

Objection Handling

Three objections you will hear in every conversation. Memorize the response.

“We already have consultants / advisors.”
This does not replace your advisor. It gives them a scored baseline. No more subjective opinions — evidence-based GTM governance. Consultants who use the GTM Intelligence Report close more follow-on work because the data proves where the constraint actually is.
“€2,495 for a report?”
You are losing 6–15% of ARR in system leakage. On a €10M company, that is €300K–€1.5M. €2,495 to find out where is the cheapest due diligence you will do this year. The report covers 12 pillars across 4 dimensions with 72 scoring engines. This is not a survey — it is a forensic analysis.
“We don’t have time.”
35 minutes. One person. No meetings, no workshops, no 6-week discovery engagement. The system does the analysis. The report is generated automatically.
“What about data privacy?”
The assessment is completed by your client directly. No third-party data access required. GRIP OS processes the self-reported inputs through 72 scoring engines. No CRM integration is required for the diagnostic.
“How do we know the benchmarks are valid?”
GRIP scores are based on constraint theory and GTM structural analysis, not crowd-sourced benchmarks. The system identifies whether a dimension is the binding constraint relative to the others — that’s structural, not statistical.
“Our procurement team won’t approve this quickly.”
The GTM Intelligence Report is a one-time €2,495 purchase. Most teams can expense that without procurement. Start there. The OS subscription decision comes after they’ve seen the results.
“The report is interesting, but do we really need the OS after?”
The report tells you what’s wrong. The OS tells you whether you’re fixing it. Without governance, constraints recur. Most clients who complete the report want ongoing visibility.

Operating Boundaries

What NOT to do

  • Do not lead with GRIP OS. Lead with the diagnosis. The GTM Intelligence Report is the entry point. Always.
  • Do not promise specific outcomes. Promise clarity. The report identifies the constraint and estimates the leakage. It does not guarantee a fix.
  • Do not position against consultants. Position alongside them. The report makes their work better, not redundant.
  • Do not share your commission structure with the client. Your commercial relationship with Caugia is confidential.
