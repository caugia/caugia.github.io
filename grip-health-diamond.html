The GRIP Health Diamond

A clear view of your GTM maturity across the four GRIP dimensions. The Health Diamond reveals strengths, weaknesses and systemic constraints.

A structured way to understand GTM maturity

The GRIP Health Diamond consolidates your maturity across the four GRIP dimensions. It helps leadership see where the GTM engine is strong, where it is at risk and which constraints matter most for growth.

It is the public face of the diagnostic engine used in the GTM Intelligence Report.

Guidance

Guidance reflects the clarity of your direction. Strong guidance means your teams share a precise understanding of your market, your value and your priorities.

Resources

Resources measure the underlying capacity of your GTM system. It evaluates data, tooling and the capability of your teams to execute consistently.

Implementation

Implementation assesses how well the core GTM processes work in practice across demand, sales and customer operations.

Performance

Performance covers the quality of metrics, forecasting and the cadence of improvement cycles. It ensures the GTM system can adapt with confidence.

How the 12 pillars map into GRIP

The diagnostic engine behind the GTM Intelligence Report uses 12 structural pillars. Each pillar belongs to one of the four GRIP dimensions.

GRIP Dimension Pillars
Guidance P1 Market Clarity, P2 Positioning and Messaging, P3 Strategy and Leadership Alignment
Resources P4 Data and Infrastructure, P5 Tooling and Automation, P6 Team Capacity and Competency
Implementation P7 Demand Operations, P8 Sales Operations, P9 Customer Operations
Performance P10 Metrics and Measurement, P11 Forecasting and Revenue Planning, P12 Review and Improvement Rhythm

How to interpret the Health Diamond

The Health Diamond provides a balanced view of maturity. High scores in one dimension cannot compensate for weaknesses in another. A strong GTM engine requires consistent maturity across all four areas.

Use the Diamond to guide your quarterly planning, to align leadership and to identify where improvement will have the most impact.
