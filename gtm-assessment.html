Frequently Asked Questions

Does the assessment require my email?

Yes. Your email lets us route your score and context into your GTM Intelligence workflow. Your result appears instantly on screen and can be used to trigger a deeper diagnostic if you decide to continue.

How accurate is the score?

The score is intentionally simple. It is not designed to replace a full GTM audit. Instead, it surfaces patterns leaders already feel, gives them language to explain those patterns and helps decide where a more detailed review is worth the effort.

What happens after I complete the assessment?

You land on a result page that explains your GTM Score and maturity band in plain language. Behind the scenes, your inputs can feed a GTM Intelligence Report or a more tailored engagement if you choose to move beyond the free assessment.

Is my data kept private?

Yes. Your inputs are used solely to generate your score and report. We do not share your strategic data with third parties.

Can I share the assessment with my leadership team?

Absolutely. In fact, having multiple leaders take the assessment often reveals misalignment gaps that are invisible in day-to-day operations.
