How the GTM Score works

The GTM Assessment evaluates the core elements of your go to market system across ten focused capabilities. Each response contributes to a raw score that is translated into a clear 0–100 maturity scale and mapped to a band from At Risk to Best in Class.

The score reflects how aligned your strategy, execution, data, and processes are and how predictable your revenue system can become as a result.

Frequently asked questions

Does the assessment require my email?

Yes. Your email lets us route your score and context into your GTM Intelligence workflow. Your results appear instantly on screen and can be used to trigger a deeper diagnostic if you decide to continue.

How accurate is the score?

The score is directionally accurate. It is designed to surface patterns leaders recognise quickly and to indicate where structure is missing or underdeveloped in your GTM system.

What happens after I complete the assessment?

You land on a result page that explains what your maturity band means and where to focus first. Behind the scenes, your inputs can feed a deeper GTM Intelligence Report if you decide to move beyond the free assessment.
