Frequently asked questions

How much can I earn?

You start at 10% and progress to 20% as you grow. An Elite partner with five GRIP OS Full clients generates up to €18,000 per year in recurring commission.

How does tracking work?

Each partner receives a unique referral link. When a prospect clicks your link, a 90-day attribution cookie is set. Any purchase made within that window is automatically attributed to you. You can track referrals and commissions in your partner dashboard.

Do I need technical skills?

No. If you understand B2B SaaS go-to-market challenges, you already have the context needed. GRIP OS is a browser-based product. There is nothing to install, integrate, or configure. We provide partner training materials and a demo workspace to get you started.

What support do partners receive?

All partners get a dedicated onboarding call, a full demo workspace, co-branded collateral, and access to the partner Slack channel. We also provide deal support for larger opportunities.

Can I white-label GRIP OS?

Not currently. Partners refer clients to Caugia directly. We handle the product, billing, and support. You focus on the advisory relationship and earn recurring revenue from every referral.
