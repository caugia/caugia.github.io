Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between the GTM Score and the Intelligence Report?

The GTM Score is a free and lightweight diagnostic. It quantifies your GTM system and shows where friction sits. The Intelligence Report is a deeper GRIP analysis. It reveals the constraints behind the score, explains why they exist and prioritises where improvement will have the highest return.

How long does the assessment take?

The assessment contains 265 questions and takes approximately 45 minutes to complete. Progress is saved automatically, so you can pause and resume. Most leadership teams complete it in one sitting.

When do I receive my report?

Your complete Intelligence Report is delivered within five working days after you complete the assessment. The report is generated using AI-assisted analysis and reviewed for quality before delivery.

What format is the report delivered in?

The report is delivered as a structured PDF document (typically 40+ pages). It includes your scores, GRIP Diamond analysis, archetype classification, priority roadmap and implementation guidance.

Is this suitable for early-stage companies?

Yes. GRIP adapts to your context and stage. Early-stage companies use the Intelligence Report to create structure and avoid common GTM mistakes. Later-stage companies use it to align teams, remove bottlenecks and scale with confidence.

Does this replace hiring a GTM consultant?

The Intelligence Report delivers diagnostic depth comparable to a traditional GTM audit, but without interviews, meetings or consulting overhead. Most companies use it as a foundation for strategic decisions and find it replaces the need for an audit.

What if I am not satisfied?

We provide fixed-scope work with clearly defined outputs. If a deliverable is incomplete, we fix it. Strategic disagreement is not a basis for a refund, ensuring a professional engagement.
