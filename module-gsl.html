GTM Strategy & Leadership. FAQ

We have a strategy deck, but teams keep doing their own thing. Will this tell us why?

Yes. The Leadership Alignment and Cross-Functional Alignment engines specifically diagnose the gap between strategy on paper and strategy in practice. They measure whether your ICP is shared across functions, whether leadership operates at the same planning cadence, and whether resource allocation follows strategic priorities or departmental inertia. If your score falls below 50, it confirms that strategy is not driving execution.

What benchmarks does this module compare us against?

The diagnostic measures ICP clarity on a 1–10 scale, strategic planning cadence (quarterly vs. annual vs. ad-hoc), cross-functional coordination frequency, and resource allocation alignment. A score below 50 across the pillar means your strategy layer is structurally disconnected from execution. High-performing GTM organizations score 70+ with weekly operating rhythm and documented ICP shared across all revenue functions.

What specific insights will the diagnostic reveal?

The 6 engines each expose a distinct layer: Strategic Clarity measures whether your GTM thesis is documented and actionable. Leadership Alignment checks if the executive team operates from one plan. Resource Allocation reveals if budget and headcount follow strategy or politics. Operating Rhythm evaluates your planning and review cadence. Cross-Functional Alignment diagnoses handoff quality between Marketing, Sales, and CS. Adaptability measures how quickly your strategy responds to market shifts.

How is this different from a leadership offsite or strategy workshop?

Workshops produce alignment in the room. This diagnostic measures whether that alignment persists in daily execution. It quantifies structural constraints. ICP clarity gaps, broken operating rhythms, misaligned resource allocation. that workshops surface anecdotally but never score. You get a repeatable measurement, not a one-time conversation.

Can 60 questions really diagnose our strategic alignment?

Each of the 6 engines contains 10 calibrated questions mapped to specific maturity indicators. The scoring methodology weights each engine equally and produces both an overall pillar score and per-engine breakdowns. This architecture means you don’t just get “your strategy needs work”. you see exactly which engine is underperforming: unclear ICP, broken cadence, or misaligned resources.
