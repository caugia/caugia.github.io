What your current shape reveals

The engine is described using four simple elements. A primary constraint, a hidden drag, a compounding risk and the first high impact fix for leadership.

  • Primary constraint: Performance is limiting the system. Until this improves, growth will feel heavy and unpredictable.
  • Hidden drag: Resources is relatively weak. This creates friction, context switching and ad hoc work arounds.
  • Compounding risk: Misalignment between Guidance and Performance amplifies every new campaign, hire and segment.
  • First high impact fix: Focus the next ninety days on a single GTM change that directly lifts the primary constraint.
