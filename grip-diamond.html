How to read the diamond

A balanced diamond means your GTM engine is healthy and predictable. A skewed shape reveals constraints. The smallest corner usually tells you where to start.

  • Guidance tells you if people know what to do.
  • Resources shows whether the system can support the plan.
  • Implementation reflects discipline and repeatability.
  • Performance confirms if the engine delivers results.
