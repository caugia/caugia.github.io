The GRIP Diamond

A clear, senior-level view of your GTM system across four dimensions. GRIP turns scattered GTM activity into a structured revenue model.

Why GRIP exists

Most GTM engines are complex. Teams grow, systems expand and decisions happen fast. Without a structured model it becomes difficult to see how everything fits together.

The GRIP Diamond provides a calm, complete view of the entire GTM system. It helps leadership align on priorities, understand constraints and plan improvements in a structured way.

Guidance

Guidance reflects how clearly your organisation understands the market and expresses its value. It sets the direction for the entire GTM engine.

  • Market clarity
  • Positioning and messaging
  • Leadership alignment

Resources

Resources represent the underlying capacity of the GTM engine. Strong guidance means little without the data, tools and people to execute.

  • Data and infrastructure
  • Tooling and automation
  • Team capacity and competency

Implementation

Implementation reflects how demand, sales and customer operations work in practice. It is the operational heart of the revenue system.

  • Demand operations
  • Sales operations
  • Customer operations

Performance

Performance ensures that decisions are informed by reliable metrics, responsible forecasting and a structured improvement rhythm.

  • Metrics and measurement
  • Forecasting and revenue planning
  • Review and improvement rhythm

How GRIP maps to the diagnostic engine

The internal diagnostic engine uses 12 structural pillars. Each pillar maps cleanly into one GRIP dimension.

GRIP Dimension Pillars
Guidance P1 Market Clarity, P2 Positioning and Messaging, P3 Strategy and Leadership Alignment
Resources P4 Data and Infrastructure, P5 Tooling and Automation, P6 Team Capacity and Competency
Implementation P7 Demand Operations, P8 Sales Operations, P9 Customer Operations
Performance P10 Metrics and Measurement, P11 Forecasting and Revenue Planning, P12 Review and Improvement Rhythm
