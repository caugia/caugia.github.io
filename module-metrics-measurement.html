Metrics and Measurement

Pillar 10 · Performance A structured review of how your organisation measures GTM performance.

What is inside this module

  • Assessment of critical KPIs and definitions
  • Review of dashboard quality and availability
  • Consistency of data used in GTM decisions
  • Recommendations to strengthen visibility

Price: €149 per module

Capabilities assessed

  • Clarity of core GTM metrics
  • Alignment between teams on KPIs
  • Quality of dashboards and reports
  • Use of leading indicators
  • Integration of data into planning cycles

Common failure patterns

  • Teams using different versions of numbers
  • Metrics that do not map to strategic goals
  • Dashboards that require manual reconciliation
  • Overemphasis on activity instead of results
  • No visibility into future risks

Impact on the GTM system

  • More accurate decisions across teams
  • Earlier detection of performance issues
  • Stronger leadership alignment
  • Better support for forecasting and planning
