Partner FAQ

Answers to the most common questions from Caugia partners.

1Can I see my client’s GRIP data?

Not directly. Client data is private. If you are working as a consultant with the client, ask them to add you as a workspace member. Partners see referral statistics and commission data in their dashboard, not client diagnostics.

2What if my client already visited caugia.com before I referred them?

First-touch attribution applies. If they visited without your referral link first, and then later click your link, your attribution takes priority within the 90-day window.

3Can I refer other partners?

Not currently. The partner program is for direct client referrals only.

4What happens if a client cancels?

Your commission stops when the subscription stops. There is no clawback on previously paid commissions.

5Can I use GRIP OS with my own clients as a consulting tool?

Absolutely. Many partners run GRIP diagnostics as part of their consulting engagements. You earn commission and deliver measurable value to your client at the same time.

6Is there a minimum number of referrals?

No minimum. Refer one client or fifty. The commission rate is the same.

7Can I get a discount for my clients?

Contact partners@caugia.com for volume pricing or custom arrangements for portfolio deals (5+ companies).

8How do I get my demo workspace?

Upon approval, you receive a full GRIP OS workspace with sample data (NovaPay). Use it to demo the product to prospects and familiarize yourself with the platform.

9What marketing materials can I use?

You can use the Partner Pitch Deck, brand assets, and refer clients to caugia.com. Do not create materials that imply Caugia endorses your services without written permission.

10Who do I contact for help?

Email partners@caugia.com or reach out to your partner contact. We respond within 24 hours.

Still have questions?

Email partners@caugia.com — we respond within 24 hours.
