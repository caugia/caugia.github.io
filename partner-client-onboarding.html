Client Onboarding Guide

A step-by-step guide to onboarding clients you refer to GRIP OS.

The Onboarding Process

1

Share your referral link

Send your unique link (caugia.com?ref=YOUR_CODE) to your client. They land on the homepage with your attribution automatically tracked via a 90-day cookie.

2

Guide them to the GTM Intelligence Report

The report is the entry point. It contains 265 questions and takes approximately 35 minutes to complete. It generates a 45-page diagnostic with GRIP scores, constraint analysis, financial impact estimates, and a 90-day resolution plan.

3

Buy for your client (optional)

You can purchase the report on behalf of your client via the Shop. Check “Purchase for a client”, enter their email, and they receive the assessment link directly. You stay invisible in the transaction.

4

Review results together

Once the report is complete, review the GRIP scores with your client. Focus on three things: the binding constraint, the financial leakage, and the first 3 recommended actions.

5

Recommend GRIP OS

After the report, guide your client to a GRIP OS subscription for ongoing constraint governance. Core (€799/mo) for self-service teams, Full (€1,495/mo) for Sophie AI advisor and the full platform including 280+ connectors, benchmarks, and the simulator.

Pro Tips

Start with the leakage number. “You are losing €X per year in system leakage” gets attention faster than any feature list.

Do not sell the product. Sell the diagnosis. The GTM Intelligence Report sells itself. Your job is to get them to take it, not to pitch features.

Frame GRIP OS as the operating system for their GTM fix. It is not another dashboard. It is the system that governs constraint resolution and tracks progress against the binding constraint.
