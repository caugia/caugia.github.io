Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between the GTM Score and the Intelligence Report?

The GTM Score is a free, lightweight diagnostic that quantifies your system friction. The Intelligence Report is a deep-dive analysis that reveals *why* those frictions exist and prioritizes the exact steps to fix them.

Who is this designed for?

Caugia is built for CEOs, CROs, and GTM leaders who want clarity. It adapts to different stages, from early scale-up to enterprise complexity.

Do we need a certain level of GTM maturity?

No. GRIP adapts to your stage. Early-stage companies use it to create structure from chaos. Larger organizations use it to align silos and remove guesswork.

Can this replace a traditional GTM audit?

In most cases, yes. The combination of the GTM Score and Intelligence Report delivers the clarity leaders seek from an audit, but faster and without the heavy consulting overhead.

Do you offer fixed pricing?

Yes. The Score is free. The Report and Modules have fixed prices. Advisory can be fixed-scope or a simple monthly retainer. We believe in pricing outcomes, not hours.
