Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between the GTM Score and the Intelligence Report?

The GTM Score is a free "pulse check" to see where you stand. The Intelligence Report (‚Ç¨1249) is a deep-dive, board-level diagnostic. It reveals the root causes, explains the constraints, and prioritises where improvement will have the highest return.

Can we start with modules without the Report?

It is possible, but not recommended. The Intelligence Report provides the context that makes each module precise. Without the diagnostic, teams often work on symptoms (e.g., "we need more leads") rather than root causes (e.g., "our positioning is unclear").

How does Advisory support work?

Advisory is for leaders who want an ongoing strategic partner. We work on a retainer basis to help you implement the GRIP system, make high-stakes decisions, and prepare for board meetings or fundraising events.

Do you offer fixed pricing?

Yes. The GTM Score is free. The Intelligence Report and Modules have fixed prices. We believe in pricing for outcomes and clarity, not for hours spent.

Is this suitable for early-stage startups?

Yes. GRIP adapts to your stage. For early-stage companies, it creates the initial structure to avoid chaos. For scaling companies, it aligns teams and removes bottlenecks.
