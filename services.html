Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is an automated diagnostic?

The Intelligence Engine uses deterministic scoring — not AI opinion. 72 diagnostic engines apply constraint priority scoring, concentration analysis (HHI), and revenue impact modelling to your specific answers. Two companies with identical answers get identical results. There is no subjective interpretation, which means there is no bias.

Why are there no consulting sessions?

Caugia is a diagnostic product, not a consulting firm. The Intelligence Engine delivers the same analytical depth a senior consultant would — without the timeline, the bias, or the billable hours. A traditional consultant brings 6 weeks and subjectivity. We bring an algorithm and minutes.

Can I buy a Deep Dive without the Report?

Yes. But the Report tells you which Deep Dive matters most. Without it, you are choosing on instinct. With it, you are choosing on data. Our recommendation: start with the Report, then let the diagnostic tell you where to go deep.

What is the difference between the GTM Score and the Intelligence Report?

The GTM Score is a free 5-minute pulse check — it shows the shape of your problem. The Intelligence Report (€2,495) is a 44-page forensic diagnostic that reveals root causes, models revenue leakage, and delivers a prioritized action plan. The Score tells you something is off. The Report tells you exactly what, where, and how much it costs you.

Can I share the Report with my board?

The Intelligence Report is designed to be board-ready. It includes an executive summary, GRIP dimension scores, system pattern analysis, and a prioritized action plan — all in language that leadership teams understand immediately.

What data do you need from me?

Only your answers. We do not require CRM access, financial data, or system integrations. The assessment is a structured questionnaire designed to surface the signal from the noise. Your answers are confidential and used exclusively for your diagnostic.
