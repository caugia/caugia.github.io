Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the Execution Partnership "Gated"?

We do not believe in ad-hoc consulting. To support a team effectively, we must first agree on the diagnosis. The Intelligence Report provides that shared model. Without it, advisory becomes reactive and inefficient.

What is the difference between the GTM Score and the Intelligence Report?

The GTM Score is a free "pulse check" to see where you stand. The Intelligence Report (€1249) is a deep-dive, board-level diagnostic. It reveals the root causes, explains the constraints, and prioritises where improvement will have the highest return.

Can we deploy System Modules without the Report?

Technically yes, but it is not recommended. The Intelligence Report provides the context that makes each module precise. Without the diagnostic, teams often work on symptoms (e.g., "we need more leads") rather than root causes (e.g., "our positioning is unclear").

Do you offer fixed pricing?

Yes. The GTM Score is free. The Intelligence Report and System Modules have fixed prices. We believe in pricing for outcomes and clarity, not for hours spent.
