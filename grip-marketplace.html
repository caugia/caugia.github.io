Frequently asked questions

Can I try it free before paying?

Yes. Lead tier includes the full vertical assessment, the binding-constraint diagnosis, three moves with owner / cost / deadline, and a 5-page PDF summary. You only upgrade when you want Sophie's continuous advisory + peer cohort.

Is there a PDF report?

The 45-page GTM Intelligence Report is available as a one-time €2,495 purchase. Every subscription tier ships with on-screen diagnosis + shareable URLs; the one-time PDF is the option when you need a formal board-level deliverable.

Do I need a vertical add-on to start?

No. Core and Full both include the base scoring engine + Sophie. Add-ons bolt on vertical-specific connectors, signals and export formats on top.

What counts as a Sophie turn?

One round-trip conversation with Sophie. We do NOT bill for turns where Sophie's claim-verification confidence falls below 0.5.

Is SOC 2 complete?

Type I audit trajectory active. 25 of 29 controls implemented and publicly auditable at /api/soc2/evidence. Type I report expected Q3 2026.

Can my data stay in the EU?

Yes. Per-workspace EU residency toggle on Enterprise. Every sub-processor on an EU-safe allowlist. Non-EU-safe providers hard-fail.
