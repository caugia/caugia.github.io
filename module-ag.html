Alignment & Governance. FAQ

Sales blames Marketing, Marketing blames Product. Will this tell us where alignment actually breaks?

Yes. The Cross-Functional Alignment engine measures whether shared goals, SLAs, and handoff processes exist between functions or if each team operates independently. The Governance engine checks if there are accountability mechanisms. documented decisions, escalation paths, and regular GTM reviews. The diagnostic reveals whether misalignment is structural (no shared goals) or cultural (goals exist but nobody enforces them).

What benchmarks does this module compare us against?

GTM review cadence (weekly target), decision documentation maturity, accountability mechanism strength, cross-functional SLA compliance, and communication rhythm effectiveness. Four or more consecutive weekly GTM reviews indicates a healthy cadence. The diagnostic also measures strategic planning process maturity, org design alignment with GTM motion, and culture indicators around collaboration vs. silos.

What specific insights will the diagnostic reveal?

Six engines cover the alignment infrastructure: Org Design evaluates whether your structure matches your GTM motion. Strategic Planning assesses planning process rigor and cross-functional input. Cross-Functional Alignment measures shared goals, SLAs, and handoff quality between teams. Governance scores decision documentation, accountability mechanisms, and escalation processes. Communication evaluates information flow and meeting effectiveness. Culture measures collaboration norms and whether alignment is performative or genuine.

How is this different from an org design consultant or team alignment workshop?

Consultants redesign reporting lines. Workshops create temporary alignment. This diagnostic measures the operating system behind alignment. whether governance structures enforce coordination, whether GTM reviews happen consistently, whether decisions are documented and tracked, and whether your culture supports or undermines cross-functional work. It quantifies alignment so you can track whether interventions actually work over time.

Can 60 questions really diagnose alignment and governance?

The 6-engine architecture covers structure through process through culture. Each engine has 10 questions calibrated against GTM governance benchmarks, from absent (no formal alignment mechanisms) to mature (systematic, measured, culturally embedded). The per-engine breakdown shows whether your alignment problem is structural (bad org design), procedural (missing governance), or cultural (no collaboration norms). each requiring fundamentally different interventions.
