Co-branding Rules

Partners may:

  • Use “GRIP OS Partner” on their website and materials
  • Use “Powered by GRIP OS” in client-facing documents
  • Use “Certified by Caugia” after completing partner onboarding
  • Reference Caugia and GRIP OS in case studies and proposals
  • Display the Caugia logo. always linked to caugia.com

Partners may NOT:

  • Use “Official Caugia Partner” without written approval from Caugia
  • Imply that Caugia endorses their services without written permission
  • Use the Caugia logo as their own logo or as part of a combined mark
  • Modify, alter, or recreate the Caugia logo
  • Display the logo without linking to caugia.com
