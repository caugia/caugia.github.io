Usage rules:

Always place the logo on white or dark (#1A2340) backgrounds. Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the “C” in Caugia on all sides. Never stretch, rotate, add effects to, or recolor the logo.

Co-branding

Partners may:

  • Use “Powered by GRIP OS” on their website and materials
  • Use “GRIP OS Certified Partner” badge (provided upon approval)
  • Reference Caugia and GRIP OS in case studies and proposals

Partners may NOT:

  • Use the Caugia logo as their own logo or as part of a combined mark
  • Imply that Caugia endorses their services without written permission
  • Modify, alter, or recreate the Caugia logo
