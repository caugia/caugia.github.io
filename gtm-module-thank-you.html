What is the difference between a Deep Dive and the full Intelligence Report?

The GTM Intelligence Report evaluates all 12 GRIP pillars (265 questions, 45-page report). A Deep Dive Module focuses on one specific pillar in depth (60 questions, 6 engines). If you are unsure where the constraint is, start with the full report. If you already know which pillar needs attention, the Deep Dive goes deeper on that specific area.
