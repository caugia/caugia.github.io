Common Questions

What is the difference between the Score and Report?

The Score is a free snapshot. The Intelligence Report is a deep 12-pillar forensic analysis revealing root causes and a prioritized roadmap.

Who is this designed for?

CEOs, CROs, and Founders of B2B SaaS companies ($1M - $500M ARR) who need to professionalize their revenue engine.

Can I buy modules separately?

No. Modules are add-ons to the Intelligence Report. We do not fix parts without understanding the whole system first.
