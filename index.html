The GRIP Diamond.

Every organisation already has a GTM system. The question is not whether it exists, but how balanced it is. The GRIP Diamond makes that balance visible in one view.

Guidance provides clarity of strategy, ICP and focus.
Without it, teams work hard but not in the same direction.

Resources represent the people, tools and infrastructure that carry the work.
When this layer is stretched or misaligned, execution becomes fragile.

Implementation is the discipline behind your motions, processes and operating rhythm.
It turns intentions into consistent behaviour.

Performance shows the outcomes across demand, pipeline, retention and expansion.
It is the visible expression of the underlying system, never the root cause.

The GRIP health card shows the strength of each force as a score. The GRIP Diamond turns that same information into a shape you can see at a glance.

The GTM Framework uses this shape as a starting point. It connects strategy, process, tools and teams into one operating system so that improvements are made in the right place and in the right order.
