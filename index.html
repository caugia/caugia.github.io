Common Questions

Before you decide

How long does the assessment take?

Most companies complete the assessment in 45 to 60 minutes. You can pause and resume before submitting. Once the assessment is submitted, inputs are locked to preserve the integrity of the diagnostic. Your report is generated and delivered within one hour after submission.

Who should complete the assessment?

Ideally completed by a revenue leader with visibility into your Go-to-Market system and performance metrics. Many companies involve their CEO or Finance for specific inputs such as monthly burn, active customers, or gross margin. The assessment is designed for leadership-level understanding of the revenue engine.

What information do I need to complete it?

Company metrics such as ARR, growth rate, win rate, churn, customer count, and team structure. Some questions involve leadership metrics such as burn rate, gross margin, or customer economics. No system integrations or data uploads are required.

How accurate is the diagnosis?

The report is generated using deterministic scoring engines across 12 GTM pillars and 72 diagnostic models. The accuracy depends on the quality of the inputs provided. When completed with leadership-level data, the diagnostic reliably identifies the structural constraint in the revenue system.

Do I need to prepare anything before starting?

Most leaders can complete the assessment without preparation. Having a few core metrics available can speed things up, such as ARR, customer count, win rate, churn, gross margin, and monthly burn. No integrations, data exports, or system access are required.

When will I receive my report?

Your full GTM Intelligence Report is delivered to your inbox within one hour of submission. No scheduling, no consultant readout, and no follow-up calls required.

Is this a subscription?

The GTM Intelligence Report is a one-time purchase. No recurring charges. GRIP OS is a monthly subscription: Core at €799/mo or Full at €1,495/mo (min. 3 months). Annual billing saves 17%. You start with the report, then optionally subscribe to GRIP OS to govern the fix.

Is this suitable for early-stage startups?

Best suited for B2B companies with an existing Go-to-Market motion and measurable revenue. If you are pre-revenue, start with the Free GTM Score to obtain a directional signal before running the full diagnostic.

What is Caugia exactly? A tool or a consulting service?

Neither. Caugia is a GTM operating system. It starts with a deterministic diagnostic (the GTM Intelligence Report) that isolates your binding constraint using 72 scoring engines across 12 pillars. Then GRIP OS governs the fix: action cascades, gates, drift detection, and an AI advisor (GRIP Brain) that knows every metric. It does not generate opinions. It does not replace execution. It identifies where your system is structurally constrained and what sequence of actions will unlock growth.

What is GRIP OS and how is it different from the GTM Intelligence Report?

The GTM Intelligence Report is a one-time diagnosis: it finds your primary constraint, quantifies the revenue drag, and builds a prioritized action plan. GRIP OS is the execution layer that comes after diagnosis. It enforces one active constraint at a time with a 272 connector library that integrates with your full GTM stack (HubSpot, Salesforce, Gong, Stripe, and more), CEO standups, board-ready reports, what-if scenario simulation, and priority resequencing when your data shifts. Think of it as: the report tells you what is wrong. OS makes sure you actually fix it — in the right order.

Do I need the GTM Intelligence Report before using GRIP OS?

Yes. GRIP OS requires a completed GTM Intelligence Report as its baseline. Without the report, the system has no verified starting constraint, no scored GRIP dimensions, and no data to sequence actions from. The report is the foundation. OS is the governance layer built on top of it.

When should I buy a Deep Dive instead of the full report?

A Deep Dive is for teams that already know which GTM dimension needs investigation. Each module runs 60 questions across 6 diagnostic engines on a single pillar. If you are unsure where the real constraint sits, start with the full GTM Intelligence Report. It covers all 12 pillars and identifies the binding constraint for you.

The Free GTM Score is a 5-minute directional signal. It gives you a high-level view of where friction might exist.
