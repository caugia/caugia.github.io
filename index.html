The GRIP Diamond.

The GRIP Diamond is the public model of the Caugia system. It turns a complex GTM engine into four clear dimensions that leadership can discuss without getting lost in tools or org charts.

  • Guidance reflects how clearly the organisation understands the market and expresses its value. It sets direction for the entire GTM engine.
  • Resources represents the data, tools and people that carry the GTM system. Strong guidance means little without the capacity to execute.
  • Implementation covers how demand, sales and customer operations work in practice. It is the operational heart of the revenue system.
  • Performance ensures that decisions are informed by reliable metrics, responsible forecasting and a structured improvement rhythm.

Every score, report and module on this site is an application of the GRIP Diamond.
