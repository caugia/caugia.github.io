The system shape across four forces

Why GTM feels heavy even when everyone works hard

Most GTM teams are not constrained by effort. They are constrained by misalignment. Strategy, execution, and measurement drift. The system becomes heavier each quarter, even when the team is strong.

Misaligned priorities

Teams optimise local metrics instead of the full revenue system. Pipeline grows, conversion does not.

No shared model

Leadership debates symptoms because the system is not visible. Decisions become slow and reactive.

Execution without sequence

Great initiatives arrive in the wrong order. The real constraint stays untouched, so effort does not compound.
