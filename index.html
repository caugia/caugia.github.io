The GRIP Diamond.

The GRIP Diamond is the public model of the Caugia system. It turns a complex revenue engine into four clear dimensions that leadership can discuss without getting lost in tools or org charts.

  • Guidance reflects how clearly the organisation understands the market and expresses its value.
  • Resources represent the data, tools and people that carry the system.
  • Implementation is how demand, sales and customer operations work together in practice.
  • Performance shows whether the system is healthy, measured and improving over time.
