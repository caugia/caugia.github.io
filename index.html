Why GTM feels heavy even when everyone works hard

Leakage rarely comes from one broken thing. It comes from a system where strategy, execution and measurement drift apart. When leadership lacks a shared model, the organization keeps shipping initiatives while the constraint stays untouched.

Local optimisation

Teams optimise their own metrics. The customer journey suffers and revenue becomes harder to predict.

Debating symptoms

Leadership debates what is visible. Without a system view, decisions become reactive and slow.

Wrong sequence

Good initiatives arrive in the wrong order. Effort increases, but it does not compound.
