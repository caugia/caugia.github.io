Frequently Asked Questions

Is the GTM Intelligence Report a subscription?

No. The GTM Intelligence Report is a one-time purchase. You complete the assessment, receive the 45-page report, and there are no recurring charges, contracts, or account management.

What is the minimum commitment for GRIP OS?

GRIP OS (Core and Full) requires a minimum 3-month subscription. After the initial period, you can cancel at any time with 30 days notice.

Is there an annual discount?

Yes. Annual billing saves 17% on both GRIP OS Core and GRIP OS Full. Contact us to set up annual billing.

Do prices include VAT?

Prices shown are exclusive of VAT. For EU-based customers, applicable VAT will be added at checkout. Non-EU customers are not charged VAT.

Can I upgrade from Core to Full?

Yes. You can upgrade at any time. Your remaining Core subscription balance is applied as credit toward the Full plan.

What currencies do you accept?

We accept EUR, USD, and GBP. Pricing adjusts automatically based on your location. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Do I need the Intelligence Report before subscribing to GRIP OS?

Yes. The Intelligence Report is the foundation. GRIP OS uses the diagnostic results to identify your primary constraint and build the governance layer around it. Without the diagnosis, there is nothing to govern.
