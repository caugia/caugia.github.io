Our Core Model

The GRIP Framework

A balanced revenue engine requires tension across four critical dimensions. When one is weak, the entire system stalls.

Guidance (Strategy)

Where are we going? Clear ICP, positioning, and strategic roadmap that aligns the entire organization.

Resources (Capacity)

What do we have? Budget, talent, tools, and content required to execute the strategy effectively.

Implementation (Execution)

How do we work? The processes, workflows, and operational discipline that convert strategy into action.

Performance (Results)

What did we achieve? The metrics, KPIs, and feedback loops that prove value and guide optimization.
