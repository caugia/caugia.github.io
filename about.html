About Caugia

Calm. Structured. Predictable. Caugia gives leaders a model to understand how their GTM system works and how to improve it with confidence.

Why Caugia exists

Many organisations work hard but lack a shared GTM model. Teams push in the right direction but without the structure needed to create stable, predictable growth.

Caugia was created to bring clarity to this complexity. The GTM Framework gives leaders a structured way to understand their current system, identify what matters most and make decisions with more confidence.

Our philosophy

  • Growth comes from systems, not slogans
  • Clarity reduces friction for every team
  • Data should inform decisions, not overwhelm them
  • Workflows matter more than tools
  • Alignment is a continuous practice, not an event
  • The best GTM systems feel calm, simple and predictable

Who is behind Caugia

Caugia Consulting was founded by Tom Meijer. Tom spent nearly a decade building GTM systems in fast growing SaaS organisations including Contentsquare and Greenly.

Across these teams he observed the same pattern. People worked hard and cared deeply about results but were forced to operate without a shared model. Tool stacks expanded faster than structure. Ownership blurred. Performance depended on individuals instead of systems.

Caugia exists as a response. The GTM Framework provides leaders with a structured way to understand how their system works, what needs attention and how to sequence change.

How we work

  • We start by understanding your context, constraints and strategy
  • We map your GTM system using the 12 pillars
  • We use data, patterns and experience to identify the most impactful improvements
  • We help you execute with clarity and alignment
