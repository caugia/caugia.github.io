The Method

The GRIP Framework

Based on the Theory of Constraints: every revenue system has exactly one structural bottleneck. Fix that, the entire system improves. Fix anything else, nothing changes. GRIP maps that bottleneck across four dimensions.

G

Guidance

Is direction clear?

Strategy, market understanding, positioning. The decisions that shape everything downstream.

R

Resources

Is the system equipped?

Pricing, product readiness, enablement. The building blocks your GTM motion depends on.

I

Implementation

Does execution convert?

Demand gen, sales execution, RevOps. Where strategy meets the market.

P

Performance

Do outcomes compound?

Retention, data quality, alignment. The forces that turn growth into durable revenue.
