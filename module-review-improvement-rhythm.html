Review and Improvement Rhythm

Pillar 12 · Performance A structured view of how your organisation learns, adapts and improves over time.

What is inside this module

  • Assessment of team level retrospectives and postmortems
  • Cadence of improvement cycles
  • Structure of GTM reviews and decision forums
  • Recommendations to strengthen learning loops

Price: €149 per module

Capabilities assessed

  • Quality of problem diagnosis and root cause analysis
  • Follow through of improvement actions
  • Cross functional learning mechanisms
  • Leadership involvement in improvement rhythm
  • Documentation and visibility of learnings

Common failure patterns

  • Retrospectives done inconsistently or without follow up
  • Issues resurfacing across quarters without structural fixes
  • No clear forum to share learnings across GTM teams
  • Leadership reviewing metrics but not processes
  • Improvements focused on individuals instead of the system

Impact on the GTM system

  • More predictable performance and fewer recurring issues
  • Better alignment and shared understanding across teams
  • Stronger culture of continuous improvement
  • More efficient use of resources and focus
